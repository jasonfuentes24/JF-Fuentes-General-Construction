Our Services

JF Fuentes General Construction specializes in providing comprehensive home renovation solutions, catering to a wide array of client requirements and tastes. Our dedication to delivering exceptional quality is a cornerstone of our approach, ensuring every project we undertake reflects this commitment. Our team of proficient craftsmen is adept at creating both aesthetically pleasing and highly functional living spaces, tailored specifically to echo your distinct style and preferences. We invite you to discover the full range of our contractor remodeling services, meticulously designed to transform your vision into reality.

Kitchen Remodeling

Transform your kitchen into a culinary paradise with our specialized kitchen remodeling services. Our construction experts excel in creating both functional and visually captivating kitchens, tailored to your desires. We specialize in renovating key elements like kitchen cabinets, countertops, backsplashes, tile flooring, and sink/light fixtures. Trust in our expertise to revitalize the heart of your home, crafting an inspiring and practical space for cooking and gathering.

Bathroom Remodeling

Achieve your ideal bathroom with our expert bathroom remodeling services. We focus on delivering comprehensive renovations, including the installation of new vanities, toilets, tiles, showers, and bathtubs. Our commitment is to create bathrooms that are not only visually stunning but also highly functional and convenient.

Basement Remodeling

Turn your basement into a versatile and engaging space with our bespoke basement remodeling services. Whether you dream of a comfortable family room, a private home theater, or a dedicated exercise area, we specialize in realizing your vision. Our team meticulously handles every aspect, from insulation and drywall to flooring and lighting, ensuring a high-quality and customized basement transformation.

Home Addition/Extensions

Expand your living space with our custom home addition solutions. Our team of builders and construction specialists is adept at creating practical and aesthetically pleasing additions, seamlessly integrating with your home’s existing design. Whether it’s additional bedrooms, bathrooms, or other spaces, we ensure each addition enhances your home's functionality and style.

Deck Building

Rely on our proficiency in framing and deck building to upgrade your home. We handle all framing requirements, including decks, sheds, extensions, and additions. Elevate your outdoor living experience with our exceptional deck building services, characterized by superior craftsmanship and meticulous attention to detail.

Home Improvement

Our expert team specializes in a wide range of services, including flooring installation that covers tile, vinyl, and hardwood to suit your preferences. We also offer professional painting services that breathe new life into your interiors and exteriors, enhancing the overall ambiance of your home. When it comes to carpentry, our skilled craftsmen ensure precision and quality in every project. From custom home additions and extensions to comprehensive remodeling, we're dedicated to enhancing your living experience. With a focus on attention to detail and a commitment to excellence, we make your home improvement dreams a reality.
