About Us

JF Fuentes General Construction is your premier destination for bespoke building and remodeling services in Washington DC, Virginia, and Maryland. Our extensive portfolio showcases our commitment to transforming homes with sophisticated kitchen remodels, luxurious bathroom renovations, and expansive home additions.

Our team, composed of industry veterans and innovative builders, ensures every project meets our stringent standards of quality and excellence. We understand your home is your sanctuary, and our mission is to enhance it with meticulous attention to detail and unparalleled structural integrity. From initial design consultation to final touches, we deliver a seamless and satisfying construction experience.

We adapt to our clients' diverse needs, offering tailored solutions that respect your vision and budget. Our transparent and collaborative approach involves you in every step of the process, ensuring the outcome reflects your personal style and objectives. With JF Fuentes General Construction, rest assured your construction or renovation project is in capable hands.

Contact us today to start the journey to your enhanced home.

Our Work Process

1. Get in Touch

Contact us today through a call, email, or by filling out our convenient Contact Submission Form.

2. Site Evaluation

Schedule a site visit with our experienced contractor to assess your project's specific needs and requirements.

3. Detailed Estimate & Consultation

Receive a comprehensive, no-obligation estimate at no cost. Our expert team calculates all project costs, including materials and labor. We offer tailored advice on material selection and design options.

4. Hassle-Free Contract

Sign it effortlessly, and we'll collaborate to set a start date that suits your schedule. Our transparent contract outlines project details, costs, and timelines.

5. Stress-Free Construction

Once the contract is signed, our experienced construction team takes over, ensuring a stress-free experience. We'll keep you informed, and you'll have direct access to our team for any questions or concerns.

6. Transformation Unveiled

When your project is complete, we'll reveal the remarkable transformation of your space. You'll have the opportunity to review our work and ensure it exceeds your expectations.
